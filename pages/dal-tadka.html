Steps:-


1. Rinse 1 cup toor dal or arhar dal (split & husked pigeon pea lentils) for a couple of times in water. Add them to a 3-liter pressure cooker. You can also cook the lentils in a pot.
2. Add 1/2 teaspoon turmeric, 1 teaspoon salt, and 3 cups water. Pressure cook for 4 whistles on high heat then lower the heat and let it cook for 3 to 4 minutes. Let the pressure release naturally.
3. In a pan, heat 1 tablespoon oil. Add 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds and let them splutter.
4. Add 1 medium chopped onion, 1 medium finely chopped tomato, and 1 or 2 chopped green chilies. Saute until the onions turn golden brown.
5. Add 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste and saute for a minute.
6. Add 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder, 1/4 teaspoon garam masala powder, and 1/4 teaspoon kasuri methi. Mix well.
7. Add the cooked dal to the pan and mix well. Add water if required to adjust the consistency.
8. Let the dal simmer for 5 to 6 minutes on low heat.
9. Garnish with 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
