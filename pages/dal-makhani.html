Steps:-

1. Soak 1 cup of whole black lentils and 1/4 cup of kidney beans overnight in enough water.
2. Drain the water and rinse the lentils and beans.
3. In a pressure cooker, add the lentils, beans, 3 cups of water, 1 tablespoon of grated ginger, and 1 tablespoon of minced garlic. Cook for 20-25 minutes or until the lentils and beans are soft.
4. In a separate pan, heat 2 tablespoons of butter. Add 1/2 cup of finely chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
5. Add 2 finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
6. Add 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1/2 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala powder, 1/2 teaspoon of coriander powder, and 1/2 teaspoon of cumin powder. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
7. Add the cooked lentils and beans to the pan and mix well.
8. Add 1/2 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 cup of cream. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes.
9. Serve hot with rice or naan.
