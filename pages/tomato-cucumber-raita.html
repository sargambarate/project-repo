Steps:-


1. In a mixing bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth.
2. Add the chopped cucumber and tomato to the yogurt.
3. Add salt, roasted cumin powder, and red chili powder to the mixture.
4. Mix all the ingredients well.
5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
6. Serve chilled with your favorite Indian dish.
