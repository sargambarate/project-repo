Steps:-


1. Rinse the bhindi in water and dry them completely with a clean kitchen towel or cotton napkin.
2. Cut off the head and tail of each bhindi and slice them into thin pieces.
3. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
4. Add chopped onions and saute until they turn golden brown.
5. Add ginger-garlic paste and saute for a minute.
6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
7. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala powder. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
8. Add sliced bhindi and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for 10-12 minutes on low heat.
9. Add kasuri methi and mix well.
10. Turn off the heat and serve hot with rice or naan.
