Steps:-

1. Soak the cashews in hot water for 10 minutes.
2. In a large pot or skillet over medium heat, add the oil, onion, garlic, ginger, and chili. Saute for 10 minutes.
3. Add the cashews, potato, tomato paste, curry powder, sugar, garam masala, coriander, and cardamom. Saute for 5 minutes longer, stirring frequently.
4. Stir in the coconut milk, yogurt, and vegetables.
5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
6. Serve hot with rice or naan.
